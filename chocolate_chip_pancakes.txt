Recipe:

- Set frying plate to 350 degrees Farenheit
- In a bowl, mix 2 cups pancake mix, 2 eggs, a half cup of milk,
  and half a cup of chocolate chips

- Once ingredients are well blended, place mix on frying 
  plate in quarter cup amounts.

- Cook on one side for 2-4 minutes, or until the bottom is 
  golden-brown in color

 - Then flip the pancake and repeat the above step for the other side

 - Once both sides are cooked, you can remove the pancake from
   the frying plate and it is ready to server!
